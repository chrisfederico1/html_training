Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Quick, easy, and tasty macaroni and cheese dish. Fancy, designer mac and cheese often costs forty or fifty dollars to prepare when you have so many exotic and expensive cheeses, but they aren't always the best tasting. This simple recipe is cheap tasty.

Ingredients for Macaroni and Cheese

  • 1 box of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

How to make it:

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil
  2. To make a roux, start by melting butter in a saucepan over mediunm heat. Add flour, salt, and pepper and stir until smooth.
  3. Add Cheese and Macaroni
